## 📚 Today's Lesson: **Why We Use Multiple Signal Sources**

One indicator lies. Two indicators can agree by coincidence. Three+ independent sources agreeing? That's conviction.

Our system uses 6 sources: AI forecasting, backtested strategies, macro regime, market data, strategy matrix, and social sentiment. Each "votes" LONG, SHORT, or NEUTRAL.

Today every crypto trade had 2/2 sources agreeing (MEDIUM conviction). That's enough to act — but we sized conservatively because it wasn't 3+ (HIGH).

**The lesson:** Don't trade on a single signal. Wait for confluence. The more independent data points that agree, the more confident you should be.
